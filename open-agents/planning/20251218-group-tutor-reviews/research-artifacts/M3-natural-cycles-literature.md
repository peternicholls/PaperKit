## 1. Twilight: The Natural Chromatic Inversion

**Relevance:** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê (DIRECT ANALOGUE TO M√ñBIUS)

**Key Findings:**

**Civil Twilight (0-30 minutes):**
- Sun: 0-6¬∞ below horizon
- Duration: ~30 minutes (varies by latitude/season)
- Colors: Warm golden tones ‚Üí cool pink tones
- Transition: "Colors changing quickly... become cooler in temperature"
- Smoothness: "Gradation becomes very smooth and pleasant"

**Nautical Twilight (30-60 minutes):**
- Sun: 6-12¬∞ below horizon
- Duration: ~30 minutes
- Colors: Deep blue with residual orange/yellow hues
- Transition: "Light dissipates quickly"
- Visibility: Horizon visible but details harder

**Astronomical Twilight (60-90 minutes):**
- Sun: 12-18¬∞ below horizon
- Duration: ~30 minutes before full night
- Colors: Minimal residual light
- End point: Night officially begins

**Total Transition:** 90 minutes (warm day ‚Üí cool night)
