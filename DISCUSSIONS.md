## Best Practices

### Be Clear and Specific
- Use descriptive titles
- Provide context

- Mention relevant agents, commands, or workflows

### Search First
Before starting a new discussion, search to see if someone has asked the same question or proposed the same idea. You can:

- Reference previous discussions
- Build on earlier conversations

### Be Respectful
- Treat all participants with respect
- Assume good intentions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Welcome diverse perspectives

### Stay On Topic
- Keep discussions focused on Paper Kit
- Use the right category for your topic

- Celebrate others' contributions
